WEATHER (ALL ROUTES ARE GET METHOD)

prefix(weather): for example: api/weather/city/hourly -- city should send from front;
1) api/weather/city/hourly -- 24 hour weather data
2) api/weather/city/25-days -- 25 days weather data
3)api/weather/city -- details of current weather


prefix(horoskopi): for example: api/horoskopi/horoscope name/love -- horoskopi should send from front;
current horoscope names are :(/verdzi,/kuro,/tyupebi,/kirchxibi,/lomi,/qalwuli,/saswori,/morieli,/mshvildosani,/txis-rqa,/merwyuli,/tevzebi)
1) api/horoskopi/[name] -- daily info of selected horoscope
2) api/horoskopi/[name]/love
3) api/horoskopi/[name]/finance
4) api/horoskopi/[name]/general
5) api/horoskopi/[name]/sexual
